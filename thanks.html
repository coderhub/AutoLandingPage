Inked Voices

Front End Developer

Who are the users?

Users ranged from 25 to 44.

What is the product?

Inked Voices is a web platform for writers. They provide tools that simplify the critiquing process.

What are the goals?

Update external and internal pages to be more responsive using Bootstrap. Add new pages to promote company services. Design iOS app to help writers keep track of their writing habits.

Development

The designs were developed using Bootstrap for a responsive layout. JQuery allowed the page to smooth scrolling between different sections.

iOS Icon

The ink On app needed a logo that would encourage users to sit down and start writing. The letter “i” in Inked Voices is incorporated into the isometric chair.

ios App Design

The iOS app generates graphs and statistics based on the users writing habits. Multiple writing projects can be tracked and goals set for each one.
